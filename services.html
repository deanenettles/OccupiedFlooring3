Our Services

Integrated Flooring Programs Built for Occupied Environments

Our services are designed for organizations that demand consistency, accountability, and transparency across single-site and national portfolios.

Ownership Program

A turnkey flooring program for owners seeking long-term value, predictable costs, and portfolio-wide consistency.

Program highlights include:

  • Multi-year planning and budget alignment
  • Standardized installation methods nationwide
  • Lifecycle tracking by asset, location, and product
  • Consolidated reporting across properties
  • Single point of accountability from planning through closeout

Mill Direct Program

We manage the process so clients can access manufacturer pricing without sacrificing control, compliance, or execution quality.

Program highlights include:

  • Direct manufacturer coordination and logistics management
  • Transparent purchasing provided as a defined line item
  • Inventory control, storage, and delivery tracking
  • Installation sequencing aligned to occupancy constraints
  • Post-installation verification and documentation

Cyclical Floor & Paint Program

A repeatable solution for facilities requiring ongoing flooring and paint upgrades in active environments.

Program highlights include:

  • Cyclical planning aligned with lease and SFO requirements
  • Coordinated flooring and painting schedules
  • Night and off-hour execution options
  • Consistent crews and quality standards
  • One integrated project management team
