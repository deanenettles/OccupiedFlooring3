Process

A Smarter Way to Renovate Occupied Space

Our Process:

  • Employees secure personal items in labeled, mobile containers
  • Workstations and cubicles are lifted—not dismantled
  • Flooring is installed efficiently and precisely
  • Spaces are fully operational the next business day

Traditional Process:

  • Multiple contractors and trade coordination
  • IT, electrical, and furniture disassembly
  • Extended downtime and lost productivity
  • Increased cost and operational risk

Why Our Process Works

Occupied Flooring Solutions reduces risk and cost by design:

  • Electrical, data, and IT systems remain in place
  • No dismantling or reinstallation of furniture systems
  • No separate movers or flooring installers required
  • Detailed phasing plans clarify scope, schedule, and sequencing
  • Inventory, storage, and logistics fully managed
  • Manufacturer-neutral recommendations based on experience
  • Integrated coordination with commercial painting partners

The result is a more efficient project, lower total cost, and minimal disruption to daily operations.
